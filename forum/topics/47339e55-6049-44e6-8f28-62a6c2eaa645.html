Dear all,

Indeed, the paper MJG points out -announcing the challenge in the Journal of Vehicle Routing Algorithms- can be cited in papers about the challenge.

We would like to point out that there will be a special issue of Networks dedicated to the VeRoLog meeting in Seville and the VeRoLog Solver Challenge 2019 (guest editors: Daniele Vigo, Ana D. López-Sánchez, Jesús Sánchez-Oro).
The corresponding Call for Papers can be found here: https://verolog2019.sciencesconf.org/data/pages/Call_for_Papers_Special_Issue_Networks.pdf.
If you’re planning to write a paper on this year's challenge, please consider submitting your paper to this special issue.

Note that a paper on the previous edition of the VeRoLog Solver Challenge recently got accepted for publication in the EURO Journal on Transportation and Logistics and appeared online last week: see https://doi.org/10.1007/s13676-019-00143-8.
